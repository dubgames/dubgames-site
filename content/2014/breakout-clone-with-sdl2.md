Tech: C, SDL2

<span style="font-size: 24px; font-weight: bold; line-height: 1;">Features:</span>

<ul>
    <li>Atari-style graphics and sounds</li>
    <li>The velocity of the ball depends on where it hits the paddle</li>
    <li>Speed increase for bricks closer to the top</li>
    <li>Scoring and lives</li>
    <li>Levels with increasing ball speed</li>
    <li>Keyboard input</li>
    <li>Cheat codes to have infinite lives and skip levels (for testing purposes)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Features that you could add:</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Make it a closer match to the Atari original</li>

    <li>Re-skin with improved graphics and sound</li>
    <li>Multi-player support (edit: added!)</li>
    <li>Add more animations and sound for level start/end, block destruction</li>
    <li>Custom levels from disk</li>
</ul>
